Indio Feliz
French-Peruvian Fusion · The local legend
The most celebrated restaurant in Aguas Calientes — a bohemian candlelit courtyard filled with vibrant murals and sculptures. French techniques meet Andean ingredients in a setting that feels genuinely special. Locals and return visitors always come back here. The atmosphere alone makes it feel like a celebration.
~$25–35/person Romantic courtyard Candlelit Reservations advised
🍽 Must order: Trout in passion fruit sauce · Aji de gallina (creamy chicken) · Creamy trout ceviche
Best for: Special occasion feel · Romantic atmosphere · Most memorable experience
🏂
TUNAWA Andean Fusion
Contemporary Peruvian · The finest food in town
Rivals Lima's finest establishments with innovative dishes and creative presentations. The six-course degustation menu is extraordinary. River views, refined service, and the most technically impressive kitchen in Aguas Calientes. If she loves creative cuisine this is her choice.
~$35–50/person River views Modern cuisine Upscale
🍽 Must order: Laguna ceviche with passion fruit · Aji de gallina ravioli (chicken) · Grilled trucha
Best for: Best food quality · Most refined experience · Food lovers

Chullpi
Modern Peruvian · Fun river terrace vibes
Great food, great views, and outstanding service on a lively river terrace. Famous for the chocolate treasure dessert that people specifically return for. The perfect choice if you want something celebratory but relaxed after a big emotional day at Machu Picchu tomorrow.
~$18–28/person River terrace Craft beer Lively atmosphere
🍽 Must order: Grilled trout with Andean herbs · Chicken lomo saltado · Chocolate treasure dessert (do not skip!)
Best for: Relaxed evening · Great dessert · Fun and casual
4️⃣
Inti House
Peruvian Classics · Warm and family-friendly
A warm, generous, no-fuss restaurant beloved by locals and families. Perfect if the day has been long and you want comfort food done really well. Great for the little one too — big portions, relaxed pace, no pretension. Chicha Morada is a must for the table.
~$12–20/person Family-friendly Comfort food Best value
🍽 Must order: Trout in maracuya sauce · Chicken aji de gallina · Fresh chicha morada for the table
Best for: Tired family · Comfort food · Best value for money
5️⃣
El Mapi by Inkaterra
Boutique Hotel Dining · Sleek and sophisticated
The most polished hotel restaurant in Aguas Calientes, inside the beautiful Inkaterra boutique hotel. Ingredients sourced directly from the Sacred Valley and Amazon. Sophisticated but approachable — the feeling of being somewhere special without full fine dining formality.
~$25–40/person Hotel restaurant Sacred Valley ingredients
🍽 Must order: Grilled trucha a la parrilla · Chicken causa rellena · Quinoa risotto with Andean herbs
Best for: Boutique feel · Quality ingredients · Sophisticated but relaxed
