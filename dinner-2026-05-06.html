La Puerta Falsa
Since 1816 · Most historic restaurant in Bogota · Ajiaco specialist
The oldest restaurant in Bogota — open since 1816. Famous for ajiaco (Colombian chicken soup with three types of potato, corn, and cream) — the signature dish of Bogota. A tiny, authentic, historic experience that no tourist guide can fully capture.
~$10-15/personSince 1816Colombian classicsHistoric
🍽 Must order: Ajiaco bogotano (chicken soup) · Tamale · Hot chocolate with cheese (Colombian tradition)
Best for: Most historic and authentic — a Bogota institution since 1816
🥈
Quinua y Amaranto
Healthy Colombian · Superfoods · Family-friendly
Fresh, healthy Colombian cuisine using superfoods like quinoa and amaranto. Famous for chicken bowls, fresh soups, and Colombian-style trout dishes. Light and clean — perfect before a long flight home.
~$12-20/personHealthyChicken & fishLight
🍽 Must order: Quinoa chicken bowl · Ajiaco (chicken soup) · Fresh lulo juice
Best for: Best for light, healthy meal before the long flight home
🥉
Restaurante El Patio
La Candelaria courtyard · Colombian home cooking
Traditional Colombian home cooking in a beautiful colonial courtyard in La Candelaria. Generous portions of chicken and fish dishes. Warm, family-run, with live music on weekends. The real Bogota on a plate.
~$10-18/personColonial courtyardHome cookingLive music
🍽 Must order: Bandeja light (chicken, rice, beans, plantain) · Grilled tilapia · Fresh fruit juice
Best for: Best for authentic Colombian home cooking experience
4️⃣
Paloquemao Market food stalls
Local market · Street food · Most authentic
Bogota most vibrant food market. Street food stalls serving fresh chicken empanadas, ajiaco, fried fish, and fresh tropical juices. Chaotic, colourful, and completely authentic. The 8-year-old will love the energy.
~$5-10/personStreet foodLocal marketMost authentic
🍽 Must order: Chicken empanadas · Fresh fried fish · Maracuya juice · Arepas con queso
Best for: Most authentic and adventurous — best for brave street food lovers
5️⃣
Andres Carne de Res Bogota
Famous Bogota institution · Massive menu · Chicken focus
One of Bogota most famous restaurants — a massive, colourful, chaotic experience. Despite the name, has excellent chicken and fish dishes. The atmosphere alone is worth it. Kids love the craziness of it.
~$20-35/personFamous institutionChicken & fishLively
🍽 Must order: Chicken lemon herb · Grilled tilapia · Colombian lemonade · Patacones (fried plantain)
Best for: Best for the Bogota experience — lively, colourful, unforgettable
